Plain English summary: Some links on this site are affiliate links. If you click one and make a purchase, we may earn a small commission — at no extra cost to you. This income helps keep the site free. It does not influence which products we feature or what we say about them.

Our Commitment to Transparency

BestPasswordGenerator.org is committed to full transparency about commercial relationships. This disclosure complies with the UK Advertising Standards Authority (ASA) guidelines, the UK Competition and Markets Authority (CMA) requirements, and the US Federal Trade Commission (FTC) guidelines for endorsements and testimonials.

What Are Affiliate Links?

An affiliate link is a unique URL that allows a third-party company to track whether a user arrived at their site via our recommendation. If you make a qualifying purchase through such a link, we receive a commission — typically a percentage of the sale price or a fixed fee per signup.

Affiliate links are clearly identified on our site in the following ways:

  • A disclosure notice appears above any section containing affiliate links
  • Affiliate links carry the HTML attributes rel="nofollow sponsored"
  • Partner product sections are labelled "Recommended Tools" or "Sponsored"

How Affiliate Relationships Affect Our Content

Our editorial independence: Affiliate relationships do NOT determine which products we feature, rank, or recommend. We will only include affiliate links for products we believe are genuinely useful and trustworthy for our readers' security needs. We will clearly state when we have NOT independently tested a product.
  • We do not accept payment to write positive reviews
  • We do not rank products based on commission rates
  • We highlight product limitations and negative findings
  • We note when a product has had a security breach or controversy
  • We update recommendations when products change significantly
